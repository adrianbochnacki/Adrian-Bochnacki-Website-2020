University

Computer Science Degree - Achieving a first-class degree in Computer Science has proven to be challenging and awarding at the same time. I have worked closely with computer security and AI robotics during my modules which helped me to develop an algorithm for the fingerprint recognition system I have used for my final year project.

Fingerprint Project

The fingerprint system has been designed to update the current proof of age system by eliminating use of personal documents. The proposed system is unique as the systems uses biometric fingerprint to identify the holder and a digital document is provided in form of QR code which anyone can read by scanning the QR code to see the holder document. The digital document is approved for authenticity. The proposed system beats the current systems on the market for being efficient and for being able to adapt world wide. I have been awarded the title of the best project of the year.
